Add-in Features

Powerful anonymization without leaving Office

In-Place Editing

Select text and anonymize it directly within your document. No need to copy-paste or switch applications.

Selection or Full Document

Anonymize selected text only or process the entire document with one click. You choose the scope.

Smart Detection

Automatically identifies 50+ entity types across 48 languages using advanced NLP models.

Multiple Methods

Choose from Replace, Redact, Hash (SHA-256), Encrypt (AES-256-GCM), or Mask based on your needs.

Preserves Formatting

Your document formatting, styles, and layout remain intact. Only the sensitive content changes.

Works Offline

Core functionality works without internet. Analysis requires connectivity for NLP processing.
