Desktop App Features

Everything you need for document anonymization

Drag & Drop Processing

Drop multiple files or entire folders. Supports PDF, DOCX, XLSX, TXT, and more. Batch process hundreds of documents at once.

Files Stay Local

Your documents never leave your device. Only extracted text is sent for analysis - files remain on your computer.

50+ Entity Types

Detect names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, credit cards, SSNs, IBANs, IP addresses, and many more entity types.

5 Anonymization Methods

Replace, Redact, Hash (SHA-256), Encrypt (AES-256-GCM), or Mask your sensitive data. Choose the right method for your use case.

Export Options

Export anonymized documents in the original format or as plain text. Generate detailed reports of detected entities.

Real-time Preview

Preview anonymization results before applying. Adjust entity detection and review changes in real-time.
