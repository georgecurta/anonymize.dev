How Tokens Work

Simple, predictable usage-based billing

What is a Token?

One token equals approximately one entity detection and anonymization. A document with 10 names, 5 emails, and 3 phone numbers would use ~18 tokens.

Rollover

Unused tokens roll over to the next month. Your balance accumulates until used. No expiration date.

Top-Up Available

Need more tokens? Purchase additional token packs anytime without changing your plan.

Track Usage

Monitor your token consumption in real-time through your dashboard. Set alerts for low balance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and your token balance is adjusted accordingly.

What happens when I run out of tokens?

You can continue using the service with reduced functionality or purchase additional token packs. We'll notify you when your balance is running low.

Is there a commitment period?

No. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Cancel anytime.

Do unused tokens expire?

No. Unused tokens roll over to the next month and never expire as long as your subscription is active.

Why is MCP Server only available for Pro and Business?

MCP Server requires real-time processing and dedicated resources. Pro and Business plans include the infrastructure needed to support this feature.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and SEPA direct debit for EU customers. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice.
